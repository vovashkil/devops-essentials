CodeStar: Team

Introduction

CodeStar creates a user as part of the initial configuration when launching a solution from a project temaplate. In addition, you can use give access to other members of your team via the Team panel. "Under the hood", the Team feature provides a simple interface to the AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM) service. In other words, you can manange users solely through the CodeStar Team feature and also go directly the users through the IAM console.

In this sublesson, you'll walk through the process of adding additional team members to your project.
