Cleanup: After you finish examining the sample application, you can clean up your sample environment resources. In the Auto Scaling console, delete the Auto Scaling group prefixed with CodeDeploy_BlueGreenDemoFleet-8ilk6o4. In the AWS CloudFormation console, delete the stack named CodeDeployBGStack-8ilk6o4. To continue the sample deployment, choose Start blue/green deployment.
